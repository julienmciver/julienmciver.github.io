About Us
About Us
Applied Welding Technology are specialists in repairing machined components by welding and we are experts in applying hard to weld layers such as Stellite.
We run a complete machine shop comprising a conventional shop with horizontal boring mills, a vertical borer and conventional lathes as well as a CNC shop that is equipped with 4 axis milling machines, lathes, spark eroder and a wire cutter.
We weld on a daily basis low alloy steels, cast iron, aluminium and stainless steels to a variety of standards such as required by large industry clients such as Eskom and Sasol and are experienced at meeting emergency repair requirements.
Notable Projects
Some of the more notable work performed at Applied Welding Technology has been:

  • The repair of 12m long internally clad pipes for Technip for the BP Block 18 development in Angola

  • Repair of the components that make up the turbine control valves (HP and IP) on GE steam turbines

  • Cladding of valve components with Stellite in a variety of sizes for Eskom and related customers

  • Repair of scroll separators with tungsten carbide

  • Cladding of nozzles and other pressure vessel equipment in sizes as small as 32mm ID
